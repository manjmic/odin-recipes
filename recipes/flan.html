Flan

What is flan?

Flan is a European and Latin dessert made up of a caramel topping and custard base that's made with milk, eggs and sweetener (which may already be included in the milk).

It is usually baked in a water bath for the best texture, and after baking it is chilled then later inverted onto a platter.

Ingredients Needed

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
